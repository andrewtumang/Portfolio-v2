About Me

I am an aspiring UX/UI Engineer passionate in the creative design and development process of web and mobile applications.

As a graduate from UC Irvine with a B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering, I have learned the engineering strategies necessary for efficient and timely development, and have specialized in several languages, including HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, C++, and Python. In addition, I have gained over 7 years of graphic designing experience with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Outside of UX/UI, you could find me tinkering with electronics, playing guitar, or trying not to lose in classic Tetris.
